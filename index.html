ABOUT

YogaPetz is a collection of 10,000 NFTs that represent a balanced life, true self and mindfulness. YogaPetz is a project focused on the well-being of the mind and body. They have a professional team to guide people to a good state of mind through meditation, yoga, breathing exercises and other activities

LIFESTYLE APP

An app will be set up that will allow you to meet, participate in classes, create your own content. Yogis (players) will be able to earn a token income by learning, practicing and exercising. The app will be open to everyone, but owners will receive bonus content and be able to participate in special events.

YOGIVERSE

Yogiverse is a place where the Yogapetz Resort will become in the metaverse. All Petz will come together to build our resort, and that will become our place for mindfulness practice through guided meditations, mindful movements and sound baths. Each of these experiences are designed to enchant your senses on all levels.
